# Project Title : Zomato Sales Analysis  
Overview :  
This project analyzes global restaurant data from Zomato to understand customer preferences, cost patterns, restaurant performance and trends across various countries and cities.  
I have created an interactive dashboard using Power BI to explore cuisines, ratings, delivery and booking trends and cost patterns.  

Dataset Description :  

Restaurant ID :	Unique identifier for each restaurant  
Restaurant Name :	Name of the restaurant  
Country Code	: Country where the restaurant is located  
City	: City of operation  
Cuisines	: Type of cuisines served  
Average Cost for Two	: Typical cost for two people  
Has Table Booking	: Yes/No  
Has Online Delivery :	Yes/No  
Aggregate Rating	: Average customer rating  
Votes	: Number of customer votes  

Dashboard Sections :  

The Power BI report contains the following visual components:
KPIs  

- Total Cuisines: 1826  
- Total Votes: 1M  
- Average Cost for Two: 1.20K  
- Total Countries Covered: 15  

Visuals Included  
- Avg Rating by Country  
- Table Booking Split (Yes/No)  
- Online Delivery Split (Yes/No)  
- Top 5 Restaurants (Count & Rating)  
- Avg Cost for Two by City
 

Problem Statements :  
- Which cuisines and cities are most preferred by customers?

- How restaurant ratings differ across countries?

- How the average cost for two varies by city or region?

- How many restaurants provide online delivery and table booking?

Insights :  
- Countries like Philippines, Turkey, and New Zealand have average ratings between 4.3 – 4.5-- indicating high customer satisfaction. India shows the lowest average rating (2.5) suggesting improvement opportunities in service or food quality.
- The dataset includes 1826 unique cuisines, proving Zomato’s wide global coverage and variety.
- 87% restaurants do not offer table booking.
- About 74% restaurants provide online delivery, making it more popular than table booking.
- Panchkula, Hyderabad and Pune are among the most expensive cities for two people.
- UAE and New Zealand have the greatest number of top-rated restaurants.

Conclusion :  

The Zomato Restaurant Analysis provides a clear understanding of global dining trends, customer preferences and restaurant performance across different countries and cities. The findings highlight how heavily customer experience, pricing and service availability influence restaurant ratings and popularity.
Key observations show that online delivery dominates over table booking, indicating a shift towards convenience-based dining. Countries like the Philippines, Turkey, and New Zealand consistently maintain higher customer satisfaction levels, while some regions demonstrate opportunities for improving food quality, service standards, or pricing strategies.
Cost analysis reveals significant variation across cities, suggesting strong differences in affordability and purchasing power.
This analysis helps stakeholders -- make informed decisions about service improvements, pricing, market expansion, and customer engagement. The interactive Power BI dashboard enhances the ability to explore these insights dynamically, enabling better data-driven strategies in the restaurant industry.
